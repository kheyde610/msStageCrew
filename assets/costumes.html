Costume Crew

Welcome to Costume Crew! We operate for every show of the year, excluding V-Show. Our meetings take place in the Costume Room and the surrounding hallway- which is in the vicinity of the orchestra room. We assemble, pull, and make the clothes and accessories that the actors wear. You will be required to run each dress rehearsal and show. You will learn the basics of hand sewing, machine sewing, measuring, and much more. No prior experience is required. We look forward to working with you soon!
