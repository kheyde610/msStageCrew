Painting Crew

Welcome to Painting Crew! We operate for every show of the year. Our meetings take place in the stage area. You will learn the basic types of painting and texturing- basing, scumbling, etc. You will work closely with the design team and construction crew to ensure that the set comes to life. You will need a set of clothes and shoes that you do not mind getting paint on. You can see some our work from past shows below. We look forward to working with you soon!
