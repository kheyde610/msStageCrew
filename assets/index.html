Welcome to Maine South High School's Stage Crew Website!

We produce 4 shows per school year - the Fall Play, V-Show, the Winter Play, and the Spring Musical. Meetings take place in the Maine South Auditorium and the surrounding classrooms in the PA-Wing. Students must commit to meeting after school each day of the school week, scheduled build/rehearsal weekends, and all performances of the shows. Crew Sign-up Forms and Safety Forms must be completed before a student joins. All forms can be found here, the Maine South Theatre Website. We hope to see you soon!
